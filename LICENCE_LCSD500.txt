LCSD500: Longitudinal Cancer Screening Dataset 
The LCSD500 is created as a derivative from the TCIA National Lung Screening Trial CT dataset and published under CC BY 4.0 licence

Please see more info and guidelines on reusing the data on https://wiki.cancerimagingarchive.net/display/NLST and further below.

Automatic lung vessel segmentations were obtained using the TotalSegmentator https://github.com/wasserth/TotalSegmentator please give credit to them as well https://doi.org/10.1148/ryai.230024 "TotalSegmentator: Robust Segmentation of 104 Anatomic Structures in CT Images".
